Power BI Project: Paris 2024 Olympic Games

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of the Paris 2024 Olympic Games, focusing on country and athlete performances as well as historical trends. Leveraging advanced Power BI features, I utilised bookmarks and buttons for seamless navigation, and incorporated calculated measures and columns to deliver precise insights. DAX formulas were employed to create dynamic calculations, enabling users to explore data from multiple perspectives.

Power BI Project: Global Invoice Analysis

This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of global invoice costs, including one-time, processing, recurring, and set-up costs. It visualises data on total costs and rebates by country, trends in monthly rebates, and cost patterns across months. Additionally, it highlights the correlation between total costs and payslip counts over time. The dashboard also includes a geographical breakdown of country costs by region (APAC, EMEA, LATAM, and North America).

Power BI Project: Spotify Analysis 2023 data

This Spotify Music Analysis dashboard provides in-depth insights into global streaming data, with a focus on total streams, track count, and average streams per track. It features visualisations of streaming trends by year, daily and monthly streaming patterns, and the performance of top tracks across major music charts, including Apple, Deezer, Shazam, and Spotify. Additionally, the dashboard highlights the distribution of tracks in various playlists and detailed track stats, including energy, danceability, and key metrics.

Power BI Project: EV USA Analysis

This Electric Vehicle Analysis dashboard offers a detailed view of the electric vehicle landscape, including total vehicle counts, average electric range, and distribution by vehicle type such as BEVs and PHEVs. The dashboard presents insights on vehicle trends over the years, highlighting growth in electric vehicle adoption. It also includes geographical data showing the distribution of EVs across various states and a breakdown of vehicles by make and model, with Tesla being the leading brand. Additionally, it displays vehicle eligibility for clean alternative fuel vehicle incentives.
