Tableau Project: British Airways Review

This Tableau dashboard provides a detailed analysis of customer reviews for British Airways. It features various metrics such as Seat Comfort, Cabin Staff Service, Food & Beverages, Inflight Entertainment, Ground Service, and Value for Money. The dashboard includes line graphs showing the average overall rating by month from 2017 to 2023, and a world map displaying ratings by country. Additionally, bar graphs illustrate the average overall rating by aircraft and seat type, as well as the number of reviews by aircraft type.
