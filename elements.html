Excel Highlight: Global Air Pollution Emissions 1900-2020

This Excel dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of global air pollution emissions from 1900 to 2022. It features a bar chart showing emissions breakdown by continent, a pie chart illustrating the percentage of emissions by continent, and a choropleth world map indicating emission levels by country. Additionally, the dashboard includes line graphs depicting total emissions per continent by decade and the top 10 countries by maximum emission decade.
